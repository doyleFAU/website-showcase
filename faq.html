Frequently asked questions about Vervio

Straight answers about our free website planning tool — what it does, who it is for, and how it compares to full website builders.

What is Vervio?

Vervio is a free website planning app. You can try fonts, colors, homepage layouts, and feature ideas in a live preview before you hire a designer or use a website builder. No login, no install, and no coding required.

Is Vervio a website builder like Wix or Squarespace?

No. Vervio is for planning, not publishing. It helps you decide what your site should look like and what it should include. Many people plan on Vervio first, then hand their picks to a developer, agency, or DIY platform.

Is there a free website planning tool for Boca Raton?

Yes. Vervio is a free website planning app that works in any browser for Boca Raton and South Florida businesses. Preview homepage layouts, try colors and fonts, and save a feature wish list — then share your plan with whoever will build your site. See our South Florida planning guide for local examples.

Is Vervio free?

Yes. Browsing presets, trying colors and fonts, saving your wish list, and walking through the Start Here tutorial are all free. There is nothing to install and no account required.

Do I need coding skills?

No. Everything is visual. Tap a style, scroll through homepage ideas, and check the features you want. The site updates instantly so you can see what you are choosing.

What is the best way to plan a small business website?

Start with your business type, pick a homepage layout that fits, choose colors and fonts, then list must-have features like contact forms, galleries, or online booking. Vervio’s Start Here tutorial walks through those steps in about three minutes.

Can I use Vervio for website planning in South Florida?

Yes. Vervio is especially useful for South Florida small businesses — restaurants, salons, gyms, real estate, nonprofits, and local shops — that want to plan their website before hiring help. Businesses in Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Fort Lauderdale, and nearby areas use it to compare homepage ideas and create a clear brief for their designer.

How many homepage layouts does Vervio include?

Sixteen ready-made homepage ideas, including layouts for tech startups, local businesses, creative studios, restaurants, fitness gyms, travel, online learning, photography, music events, medical practices, nonprofits, and more. Browse them on the Home Pages section.

How is Vervio different from hiring a web agency?

Vervio helps you arrive prepared. Instead of guessing what you want, you can show a developer or agency exact colors, fonts, layouts, and a feature checklist. That saves time and often reduces back-and-forth on the first draft.
