Questions & answers

Visitors can tap to expand each answer.

How long does a website take?

Many first sites are ready in 1–2 weeks once photos and text are ready.

Can I change things later?

Yes. Most sites are built so you can update text and images over time.

Will it work on my phone?

Yes. Modern sites automatically fit phones, tablets, and computers.

Do I need to write everything myself?

No. Many people start with bullet points and photos — your site builder can help shape the words.

Can customers book or pay online?

Yes. Booking, contact forms, and online payments are common add-ons you can include later.

What is a domain name?

That is your web address — like yourbusiness.com — so people can find you easily.
